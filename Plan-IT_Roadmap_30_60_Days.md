# Plan-IT: 30 / 60 Day Roadmap

## PHASE 1: 30 Days

**Goal:** Establish the core productivity engine, secure login, and baseline reporting.

### Key Deliverables:
- **Core Login Setup**: Integrated Microsoft OAuth (Name, Email, Photo, Dept).
- **Dashboard**: At-a-glance view of current tasks, overdue items, rank, and weekly XP.
- **Task System**: Gamified tasks with difficulty and urgency based XP calculations.
- **Projects Module**: Centralized timeline, milestones, and member management.
- **Resource Upload**: Document storage for design files, code docs, and QA reports.
- **Reports Export**: Basic one-click Excel and CSV exports for HR.

---

## PHASE 2: 60 Days

**Goal:** Scale employee engagement, culture building, and advanced AI features.

### Key Deliverables:
- **Anonymous Feedback Launch**: Safe, secure text posts for work culture suggestions.
- **Performance Score Engine**: Intelligent scoring based on speed, deadline adherence, quality, and collaboration.
- **Employee of the Month Automation**: Auto-selection based on total XP and rank stability.
- **Community Feed**: A social-media style feed for sharing wins, clapping, and appreciating teammates.
- **Notifications**: In-app and push alerts for task approvals and tags.
- **Mobile Optimization**: fully responsive PWA.
- **HR Analytics**: Advanced graphical reporting tools inside the dashboard.
- **AI Suggestions**: Smart productivity trend analysis for individual employees.
